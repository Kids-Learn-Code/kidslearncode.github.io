Skills Developed

  • Confidence: Students are taught how to make slides and present them in our workshops.
  • Originality: Students are encouraged to come up with new ideas using the problem statement.
  • Digital Literacy: Students learn how to stay safe and code better with Computational Thinking.
  • Engagement: Quizzes and hackathons keep students focused and satisfied during workshops!
