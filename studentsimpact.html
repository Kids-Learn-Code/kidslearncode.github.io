IMPACT: Empowering Students from All Backgrounds

Yearly, Kids Learn Code organises a free coding workshop to help students from all backgrounds who are interested in learning coding to join us for our virtual workshop. This virtual workshop is conducted by experienced student trainers, ensuring that learning is fun through engaging quizzes and interactions.
