How are we different from other coding providers?

#1: Engaging Quizzes for Testing Understanding

We have frequent checkpoints in the form of Kahoot/Slido Quizzes not just to allow students to check on their understanding, but also to allow them to rest and recharge while competing with their friends and other students.
The questions are based on the class' content and are also a way for us to adjust the workshop and focus on weaker topics should there be questions that the class does not do well in.
