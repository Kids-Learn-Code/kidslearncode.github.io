Meet Our Team

Since young, Liang Tze has been passionate about using technology, education, and creativity to drive meaningful change. As the Founder of Kids Learn Code, he has empowered over 200 students—including underprivileged youth—by teaching them coding skills and encouraging innovation. His work helps young learners build confidence, spark creativity, and believe in their potential to make a difference. At the School of Science and Technology (SST), he has developed strong skills in project management, team leadership, and creative problem-solving. His journey includes achievements such as being a Top 10 Finalist in Samsung Solve for Tomorrow 2023, receiving the Design Excellence Award at ARTC, and earning accolades in various STEM and environmental competitions. Beyond academics, Liang Tze actively contributes to the community through volunteering with organisations like the National Library Board, and was shortlisted for the 2024 President’s Volunteer and Philanthropy Award in recognition of his efforts. Professionally, he has taken on impactful roles such as leading tech deployments during his internship at Brahm Centre Ltd and organising workshops under Kids Learn Code. Through these experiences, he blends technical know-how with a collaborative mindset to lead initiatives that drive both innovation and social good.
